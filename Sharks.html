What is Sharks?

Like many games of the Atari era the game is simple, you a swimmer ship wrecked in shark infested waters. The goal? survive as long as you can

Upcoming Features

Or the Player may choose from Easy or Hard mode from the Difficultiy Switches
Obstacles
Objects such as Drift wood from shipwrecks will appear impeading the Players progress
Weather
Rough waters make the current more difficult to swim in
Improved ScoreBoard
The score board only displays two digits

How to Play

Playing Sharks is simple move from left to right to advance your score on the left, the color of the water will change as you progress As you get eaten the number on the right will increase
